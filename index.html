Workly is a revolutionary real-based job platform that instantly connects freelancers, service providers, and businesses through seamless job posting, service marketplace discovery, and geo-located matching.

Platform Features

Complete end-to-end solution for modern job matching and services.

Instant Job Posting

Create job postings with custom tags (Full-time, Part-time, Online, On-site) and category tags (IT, Engineering, Design, etc.) in seconds.

Reels Job Discovery

Scroll through jobs as short-form reels. Apply with one tap, bid on projects, and connect instantly with employers.

Geo-Located Map View

See all jobs on an interactive map with their locations, distance, and availability indicators.

Service Marketplace

Create service profiles showcasing your skills. Others can request your services, and you manage all offers in one dashboard.

Explore & Filter

Advanced filtering by job type, category, location, salary, and experience level. Search for jobs or services by tags.

Smart Matching

View applicant profiles with ratings, skills, bio, and tags. Accept or reject with intuitive swipe mechanics.

Direct Communication

Call, email, or message accepted candidates directly. Manage all communications from the dashboard.

Rating & Reviews

Build your professional reputation with ratings and reviews from employers and clients.

Key Architecture Decisions

  • → Cross-Platform First: Expo/React Native enables single team to build iOS & Android simultaneously, reducing time-to-market.
  • → Real-Time Database: Supabase provides instant updates for job postings, applications, and notifications without polling.
  • → Geo-Location at Core: Google Maps integration enables job discovery by location, a key competitive feature.
  • → Type Safety: TypeScript prevents runtime errors and improves team collaboration on shared codebase.
